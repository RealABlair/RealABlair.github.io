Freaky Tanks

Freaky Tanks is a singleplayer/multiplayer tank game. You can select a tank color, start a bot game or a multiplayer lobby. Also there are some weapons to choose from. A simple cannon and a ricocheting railgun cannon.

Kapitoshka

Kapitoshka is a game about living water droplet, that must escape the kitchen full of insects and other hazards.
